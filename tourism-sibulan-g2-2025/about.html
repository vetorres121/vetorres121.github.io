Welcome to Sibulan

A town rich in culture, natural beauty, and vibrant community life

Overview

Sibulan is a picturesque coastal town in Negros Oriental, known for its warm and friendly community, breathtaking coastal views, and rich cultural heritage. With its serene beaches, scenic public plazas, and historic churches, Sibulan offers a unique blend of nature, history, and tradition that makes it a charming destination for both residents and visitors.

Culture & Community

The town is deeply rooted in Filipino traditions, with local festivals and cultural gatherings celebrated throughout the year. Visitors can experience the town’s vibrant community spirit at the public plaza, local markets, and during various festivities, which showcase the talents and creativity of its people.

Natural Attractions

Sibulan is blessed with natural beauty, including pristine coastal areas, lush greenery, and scenic landscapes. Casa Mira Coast provides a peaceful residential area by the sea, while nearby natural spots allow for relaxation, photography, and eco-tourism activities. Its proximity to Dumaguete City also makes it a convenient gateway for exploring other parts of Negros Oriental.

What Sibulan Offers

Whether you are visiting for leisure or planning to stay, Sibulan offers a welcoming environment, local delicacies, affordable accommodations, and various attractions that cater to all types of travelers. The town continues to grow as a hub for culture, tourism, and community development, while maintaining its traditional charm and warmth.

Plan Your Visit

Explore the town’s key landmarks, enjoy the local cuisine, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of Sibulan. The town invites visitors to discover its hidden gems and experience a true taste of Negros Oriental’s coastal lifestyle.
